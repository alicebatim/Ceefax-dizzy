101 - Start to Spade

Screen 1: Dizzy starts at home. Move right to exit.

Screen 2: Pick up the key on the left platform.

Screen 3: Jump over pit, carefully avoid enemy.

Screen 4: Near the wall, pick up the spade.

Tip: The spade is required to clear obstacles later in the mine.

102 - Mushroom Cave

Screen 5: Enter the cave, avoid mushrooms that move back and forth.

Screen 6: Jump across small platforms.

Screen 7: Collect safe mushrooms for points.

Screen 8: Watch out for falling rocks, use timing to cross to the exit.

103 - Bridge Area

Screen 9: Approach the broken bridge, push crate to extend it.

Screen 10: Jump gaps carefully.

Screen 11: Avoid enemy and pick up rope at far end.

Screen 12: Use rope to descend to next area.

104 - Mine

Screen 13: Enter the mine entrance, obstacles block the path.

Screen 14: Use spade to dig through piles of rocks.

Screen 15: Navigate moving mine carts.

Screen 16: Pull the lever to unlock exit door.

Screen 17: Exit mine to next area.

105 - Final Area

Screen 18: Place collected items in correct spots to solve puzzle.

Screen 19: Avoid enemies and timed traps.

Screen 20: Reach final chest to complete the stage.

Congratulations! Walkthrough complete.
